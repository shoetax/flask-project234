Privacy Policy

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

At ShoeTax, we believe in simple, transparent data practices. Unlike many other tools, we've designed our email marketing solution to respect your privacy by default.

What We Don't Do

No Account Creation: We don't require you to create an account or login. Your information stays on your device.

No Data Collection: We don't collect, store, or transmit your email lists, credentials, or any personal information to our servers.

No Tracking: There are no hidden trackers, analytics, or monitoring of your usage patterns.

How Your Data is Handled

Local Processing Only: All email sending operations happen directly from your computer using your own Gmail account.

Temporary Storage: Your email lists and credentials are only stored locally during your active session and are never saved permanently.

No Third-Party Sharing: We don't share any data with advertisers, affiliates, or other companies.

Your Responsibility

While we've designed ShoeTax to be privacy-focused, please remember:

Gmail's Policies: You're subject to Google's terms of service when using their email platform.

Anti-Spam Laws: You're responsible for complying with email regulations like CAN-SPAM in your country.

Data Security: Protect your own device with antivirus and security best practices.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever make changes to how we handle data, we'll clearly notify users through the application interface. Our fundamental principle of not collecting your data will remain unchanged.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about our privacy practices, please contact us at help.shoetax@gmail.com.
