About Our X-Ray Search Tool

Our X-Ray Search Tool is a powerful solution designed to help professionals find contact information across various social media platforms and websites. Unlike traditional search methods that often yield irrelevant results, our tool generates precise Google search queries (known as "dorks") that target specific platforms and criteria.

The tool works by leveraging Google's advanced search operators to filter through publicly available information. When you enter parameters like job title, location, or company domain, the tool constructs a search query that pinpoints profiles containing that exact information. This technique is particularly valuable for recruiters, sales professionals, marketers, and researchers who need to find specific types of people.

What makes our tool special is its ability to combine multiple search parameters into a single, optimized query. For example, you can search for "marketing managers in Chicago who work at companies with a specific domain" with just a few clicks. The tool automatically adds the necessary search operators and structures the query for maximum effectiveness.

We've designed the interface to be intuitive while still offering powerful capabilities. The platform selector lets you target LinkedIn, Twitter, Facebook, Instagram, or YouTube with one click. Additional fields let you refine your search further, and the generated query can be copied or searched directly with Google.

It's important to note that this tool only finds information that is already publicly available. We don't scrape data or bypass any platform restrictions. The results you get are the same as what you'd find manually searching Google, but with much greater efficiency and precision.

Whether you're building prospect lists, searching for job candidates, conducting market research, or trying to reconnect with old colleagues, our X-Ray Search Tool can save you hours of manual searching while delivering better results.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does this contact finder tool work?
Our tool generates advanced Google search queries (called "dorks") that help you find professional contact information across social media platforms and websites. These queries search for publicly available information using specific search operators and keywords you provide. The tool combines your inputs with platform-specific search parameters to create highly targeted queries that return more relevant results than standard searches.
Is this tool legal to use?
Yes, the tool only helps you find information that is already publicly available on the internet. However, how you use this information may be subject to various laws (like GDPR, CCPA) depending on your location and purpose. Always use contact information ethically and in compliance with applicable laws. We recommend reviewing the terms of service for each platform you're searching and being transparent about how you obtained contact information when reaching out to people.
Why aren't I getting many results?
Try these tips for better results:
  • Use more specific job titles or keywords (e.g., "Digital Marketing Manager" instead of just "Marketing")
  • Include a location to narrow results (especially for common names)
  • Try different combinations of search terms
  • Verify you're searching the right platform for your target audience
  • For LinkedIn, try variations of job titles that might appear in profiles
  • Remove some filters if your search is too restrictive
Can I save my search queries?
Currently, the tool doesn't have a built-in save feature, but you can copy your generated queries and save them in a document or note-taking app for future reference. We're working on adding account features with save functionality in a future update. For now, we recommend organizing your successful queries in a spreadsheet with notes about when and how you used them for future reference.
How accurate are the email addresses found?
The accuracy depends on the source of the information. When searching for email addresses, our tool looks for patterns like "email@domain.com" or "mail:domain.com" in publicly available content. However, not all results will be current or correct. We recommend verifying any email addresses you find using free tools like MailTester or Hunter's email verifier before using them for outreach. For best results, combine email searches with other identifiers like name and company to ensure you're contacting the right person.
What's the difference between this and LinkedIn's built-in search?
LinkedIn's native search has limitations unless you pay for premium features. Our tool creates searches that can find profiles LinkedIn's algorithm might hide or deprioritize. It also allows you to search for specific content within profiles (like email addresses) that LinkedIn's search doesn't support. Additionally, our tool can search across multiple platforms simultaneously, while LinkedIn only searches its own database.
